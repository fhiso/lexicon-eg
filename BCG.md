##BCG##

\[[BCG](http://www.bcgcertification.org)\] Board for Certification of Genealogists: Since 1964, a non-profit Board that has articulated American standards for genealogical practice and (internationally) conducts rigorous testing of those who seek credentials as a Certified Genealogist. The most-recent edition of its standards manual is Genealogical Standards (2014).
