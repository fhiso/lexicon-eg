##markup (UK: mark-up)##

\[[Wikipedia]() 2014-10-03\] A (document) markup language is a modern system for annotating a document in a way that is syntactically distinguishable from the text. The idea and terminology evolved from the "marking up" of paper manuscripts, i.e.  the revision instructions by editors, traditionally written with a blue pencil on authors' manuscripts.
